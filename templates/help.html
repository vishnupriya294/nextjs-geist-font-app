Frequently Asked Questions

Getting started is easy:

  1. Create an account by clicking "Sign Up"
  2. You'll receive $10,000 in demo money to start trading
  3. Browse available stocks in the "Trade" section
  4. Buy your first stock and start building your portfolio

No, this is a demo trading platform. All transactions are simulated and no real money is involved. This platform is designed for:

  • Learning how stock trading works
  • Practicing investment strategies
  • Understanding market dynamics
  • Building confidence before real trading

To Buy Stocks:

  1. Go to the "Trade" section
  2. Browse or search for stocks
  3. Click "Buy" on your desired stock
  4. Enter the number of shares
  5. Review and confirm your order

To Sell Stocks:

  1. Go to your "Portfolio"
  2. Find the stock you want to sell
  3. Click "Sell"
  4. Enter the number of shares to sell
  5. Confirm your sell order

Your portfolio performance is tracked automatically:

  • Dashboard: View your total portfolio value and recent transactions
  • Portfolio Page: See detailed holdings, gains/losses, and allocation
  • Real-time Updates: Stock prices update automatically
  • Performance Metrics: Track percentage gains/losses for each holding

The stock prices are simulated for demo purposes. While they're based on real company tickers and approximate real market values, they:

  • May not reflect actual current market prices
  • Are updated periodically with simulated changes
  • Are designed to provide a realistic trading experience
  • Should not be used for real investment decisions

If you spend all your demo money:

  • You can still sell your existing holdings
  • You won't be able to buy new stocks until you have sufficient balance
  • Contact support if you need your demo balance reset
  • This is a great learning opportunity about risk management!
